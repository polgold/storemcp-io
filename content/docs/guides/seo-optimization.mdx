## Full-site SEO audit

```text
"Audit every page, post, and product. For each, report: meta title length,
meta description length, presence of focus keyword in title, and noindex flag.
Output as a table."
```

The AI iterates `store_mcp_list_*` + `store_mcp_get_seo`.

## Fix everything flagged

```text
"For every product flagged in the audit with a missing meta description,
write one based on the product name and short description. Keep under 155
characters. Spanish."
```

## Yoast or RankMath?

StoreMCP auto-detects which SEO plugin is active and writes to the correct meta keys. You never need to specify.

## Internal linking

```text
"Suggest 5 internal links from the blog post 'Top 10 Italian pastas' to
relevant products or category pages."
```

## Structured data

```text
"Which products are missing schema.org Product markup? Fix them by setting
the Yoast schema piece for each."
```

## Tips

- Run an audit monthly — small drift compounds
- Keep descriptions tailored to the language and market, not machine-generated
- Flag auto-generated content with a tag for future review
