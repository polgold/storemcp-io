## Activate

**StoreMCP → License → Enter key → Activate**

The site phones storemcp.io to validate the key. Once activated, Pro/Agency features light up immediately.

## Deactivate / transfer

**StoreMCP → License → Deactivate this site**

Deactivation frees a slot so you can activate on another site. Transfer is instant.

## Renewal

Licenses auto-renew annually. Turn off auto-renew any time from your [account dashboard](/account). You'll get email reminders 30 / 7 / 1 days before renewal.

## Grace period

If renewal fails (expired card, etc.), you get a **14-day grace period** where Pro features keep working. After that, Pro features deactivate — your data and settings are never touched.

## Offline / air-gapped

Contact us for offline license files (`.license`) — used for sites that can't reach the internet.
